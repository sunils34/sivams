About Siva

Siva Microbiological Solutions, LLC is located in a new facility established in the Keystone Innovation Zone in Bucks County at 360 George Patterson Blvd, Research and Development Business Park, Bristol , PA 19007. Our new facility is furnished with state of the art equipment needed for evaluating microbial problems, including stereo and phase contrast microscopes, leaching equipment, incubators, humidity cabinets, algae growth chambers, etc.

Scientists in our group have a long history and combined experiences of over 75 years studying, researching and working on biological and chemical systems including Botany, Mycology (Fungi), Phycology (algae), Microbiology, Organic Chemistry, Biochemistry and Molecular Biology and have extensive industry experience in microbiology of materials, paints, coatings, consumer products, wood, plastics etc. and in molecular biology with expertise in PCR technology for molecular diagnostic applications.

SivaMS has teamed up with New Jersey based NCS Pharmaceuticals, a Contract Research Organization (CRO) which has expertise in a wide range of analytical techniques across both the pharmaceutical and specialty chemicals areas. Dr. Jeffery Hinkle, Ph.D., MBA, responsible for business development at NCS, has graciously agreed to serve as the senior business and technical consultant for SivaMS. Dr. Hinkle and Dr. Lakshmi Sadasivan were former colleagues at Hüls America working on microbial problems of paints and coatings, as well as biocides discovery and evaluation programs. Dr. Hinkle was previously chairman of the ASTM D01-28 subcommittee on Bio deterioration of paints and coatings for several years. Together, Lakshmi and Jeff have worked on the development of, and participated in round robin evaluations for, several new and improved standard test methods such as ASTM D-5590, ASTM D-5589, ASTM D-5588, ASTM D-2574, and ASTM D-3273.

Now by teaming together, the outstanding combination of NCS and SivaMS brings extensive knowledge and expertise of microbiology, coatings, colorants and analytical capabilities to serve the market excellently. We look forward to partnering with you to solve your problems and meet your needs!

Services

Lakshmi Sadasivan Ph.D

Founder

Lakshmi is a self motivated technical sales microbiologist in the area of biocides, antimicrobials and biodeterioration of materials. She has worked on research and development projects for successful introduction of products for antibacterial, antifungal and antialgal properties. She has held a number of technical training and web seminars conducted on the subjects of plant hygiene, mold and mildew problems, and test methods. Her expertise includes the preservative efficacy testing, antimicrobial evaluations for dry surfaces and coatings, bacterial, fungal and algal identifications. She has provided many technical value propositions, worked and written over twenty technical research reports for the business and published several articles in trade and peer reviewed journals.

Prior to founding SivaMS, Lakshmi was a Senior Scientist (Group Leader) in the Coatings Biocides Group in Rohm and Haas Company and managed groups of technicians and scientist for successful completion of projects resulting in significant business, sales and recognition for Rohm and Haas. She supported product stewardship and regulatory team for successful registration of products with the EPA. She has played a key technical role in obtaining EPA registration of approximately 10 products. Lakshmi has represented Rohm and Haas in several professional and industrial societies and associations like ASTM, IBRG, and nominated in the Organization of Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) for the sub- group on the efficacy of biocides and antimicrobials to treat articles.

Lakshmi has traveled globally to visit international customers and promoted products. She has offered over fifty technical trainings and presentations to customers including web seminars on varied microbiological topics of their interest. Dr. Sadasivan has also received several awards and recognition for the outstanding service and contributions to the organization.

Dr. Sadasivan’s prior assignment was at Huls America (now Degussa/Evonik). As a Sr. Microbiologist, she conducted research in the discovery and screening of biocides for paints and coatings application. She has promoted products to customers and trained sales and marketing forces.

Before transitioning to industrial opportunities, Dr. Sadasivan’s was in academia as a post-doctoral research associate in the microbiology and biochemistry department at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ. As a post-doctoral research associate in microbiology she has several papers in peer reviewed journals like J Bacteriology on topics on bacterial flocculation, survival and cyst formations. Isolated and characterized antifungal compounds from bacteria antagonistic to plant pathogens resulting in improvement of turf grass production through biological control of fungal pathogens of golf courses. (US Patent # 5589381).

Jeffery Hinkle Ph.D, MBA

Senior Consultant

Dr. Jeffery S. Hinkle is a founding partner of the Sigfreit Group, a senior level consulting and business representation firm, and also the NCS Pharmaceuticals contract research organization (CRO) division. Dr. Hinkle recently has rejoined a past colleague Lakshmi Sadasivan helping develop and grow the new business, Siva Microbiological Solutions (SivaMS) as a senior consultant. SivaMS offers expert and cost effective solutions for any microbial problems, particularly in coatings, but also in other areas. NCS provides expert, cost effective solutions for analytical and bioanalytical testing requirements in the pharmaceutical, environmental, coatings and specialty chemical fields. Together, NCS and SivaMS have teamed up to offer broader solutions to the market.

Jeff was previously a Senior Director in the Research Planning and Integration (RPI) group of Merck Research Laboratories (MRL) responsible for the creation and optimization of the global basic and pre-clinical development sourcing efforts, development of the partner relationship management (PRM) system, and the Merck partner relationship executive for WuXi (a Shanghai-based global CRO). After leaving Merck, Dr. Hinkle provided a consultation role at WuXi as the partner relationship manager for Merck, and helped establish their business development (BD) group and customer relationship management (CRM) system.

Dr. Hinkle's prior accomplishments at Degussa (now Evonik) included identifying a bankruptcy acquisition, incorporating that business into an existing facility, developing and implementing the business, marketing and IP strategy to successfully turnaround the business during an expatriate assignment in the Netherlands. In addition to the antimicrobial, cardiovascular, and oncology pharmaceutical therapeutic areas, as well as Biotech, contract research and contract manufacturing, his core expertise extends into other application areas including primarily paints, coatings, plastics, cosmetics, inks, and ink jet among others.

Jeff's functional area expertise includes customer support, technical service, product development, process support and scale-up, key account management, marketing, business development, M&A, P&L and strategic sourcing. He is a lean six sigma black belt, and has identified, developed and completed projects generating cost savings ranging from $1.8 to over $10 million each.

Dr. Hinkle obtained his B.S. in chemistry from Ursinus College, and his Ph.D. in organic chemistry from Princeton University under Ted Taylor working with aza-beta-lactam analogs of antibiotics, and supporting chemistry for a project which is now the marketed oncology drug Alimta. He completed his MBA at Rutgers University School of Business in 2002 with a dual Marketing and International Business major. He is a member of ChemPharma, the Marketing Executives Networking Group (MENG), the American Chemical Society (ACS), Sigma Xi, the American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM, past subcommittee chair), the National Paint and Coatings Association (past Federation of Societies for Coatings Technology Publications Committee), and the Professional Services Group of Central NJ (PSGCNJ).

Easwara Sadasivan Ph.D

Vice President and Managing Partner

Dr. Easwara I. Sadasivan has been at St. Lukes-Roosevelt Hospital (NY) for the past 10 years as an American Society for Clinical Pathology (ASCP) certified and New York State licensed Molecular Pathologist. He holds strong expertise in molecular technologies such as Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR)-Amplicor viral load quantification for human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) (Roche Diagnostics), branch DNA (bDNA) System 440 (Siemens Diagnostics) viral load for HIV and Hepatitis C Virus (HCV). He performs all troubleshooting and maintenance work of laboratory equipments, daily quality control (QC) monitoring, half-yearly testing of College of American Pathologists (CAP) and New York State Surveys for Laboratory Accreditation as required for Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services. He does Annual Viral Load monitoring exceeded more than 15,000 specimens from 6 Medical Centers from metropolitan New York . He also performs PACE2C (GENPROBE) molecular screening and confirmation tests for intracellular obligate bacterial ribosomal RNA detection in sexually transmitted diseases (STD) such as Chlamydia Trachomatis and Neisseria Gonorrhoea.

In addition, Easwara holds significant responsibility and experience in serological diagnostics, which includes STD Syphilis for Treponima Pallidum spirochete bacterium tests by Rapid Plasma Reagin (RPR), Venereal Disease Antigen Test (VDRL), Immuno florescence test FTA , Cr. Neoformans (Fungus) identification by Cryptococcal Antigen latex bead test both for Serum and Cerebal Spinal Fluid, serum test Infectious Mononucleosis, Enzyme-linked Immunoassay (EIA or ElISA) for detection of both IgG antibodies for H.pylori, Measles, Mums, Rubella, Varicella, Toxoplasma Gondii (IgG. and IgM.), Cytomegalo virus (IgG. and IgM.) , Epstein-Barr Virus (IgG. and IgM.), Lyme disease (IgG.) and autoimmune IgG. SSA, SSB, Smith, smith RNP and Treponima Pallidum spirochete bacteria (IgG).

Previously, Easwara was an Assistant Professor at the State University of New York Health Science Center at Brooklyn for 16 years, a Research Associate and recipient of the National Research service Award winner from National Institute of Health (NIH) (for 4 Years), and held supervisory experience of training Technicians, Medical Students, Residents and Fellows at Hematology and Oncology at Veterans Administration Medical Center and at University Hospital. The research work he did focused on Molecular Cloning of complimentary DNA (cDNA), multifamily gene characterization, regulation, expression and purification of human folate receptors. He is a Technical expert in guiding in other on going projects in the Medicine Department. This research work was reported in 16 publications in peer-reviewed journal. Easwara was an invited speaker in National and International meetings. During this time, Easwara developed Epoxy activated affinity matrix , purification, setting up of the molecular cloning, DNA sequencing, gene expression, nucleic acid hybridization such as southern and Northern Blots, Gene Array Technology for folate receptor.

Dr. Easwara I. Sadasivan obtained his Ph.D from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst. He has a post graduate Masters degree in Molecular Biology from Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) [affiliated to the University of Bombay] and B.Sc degree in Chemistry from the St. Alberts College affiliated wit the Kerala University.
